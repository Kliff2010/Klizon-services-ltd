ABOUT US

KLIZON SERVICES LIMITED is a multi-discipline engineering service company in Nigeria. We offer services in pipeline fabrication and installation, Installation of medium and high voltage switchgears, Procurement of Oil and Gas Tools, HV and LV panel installation, maintenance and operations. We are fully registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) in the Federal Capital Territory of Nigeria, Abuja with a large and equipped workshop at our disposal. We provide our clients excellent services in various capabilities and also provide Procurement Services to the Oil and Gas industry. Our experience allows us to be extremely responsive to the execution of projects and we are able to pursue aggressive schedules and maintain very competitive pricing. We pride ourselves in positive client feedback regarding the timeliness and quality of our services. This flexibility allows us to serve a wide variety of client’s from small to major companies. We have proven capabilities together with our experience and qualified engineers who “think safety first” in the execution of their job. KLIZON SERVICES LIMITED enjoys talented workforce, which are firmly committed to client’s business now and beyond
